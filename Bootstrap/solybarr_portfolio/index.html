About

Hello, my name is Sol and I am a web developer born and raised in Central America.

I taught myself about the WWW when it was just starting out back in 1996, and it still excites me because of the new tools and breakthroughs!

My work experience consists of a mix of Web Development Consulting and Freelance Translating Services (Spanish <> English).
When working as a Web Dev consultant I’ve undertaken tasks such as:

  • Adapting purchased themes and templates to Clients specs
  • Evaluating plugins, themes templates and software for client projects
  • Installing and adapting scripts (PHP, Javascript), Creating custom software (ASP/VbScript, PHP).

As a freelancer interpreter and translator my tasks included mainly working with printed and digital translations such as manuals, websites, brochure and pamphlet translations and more.

My Resume

Here are some of the most frequent questions clients have for me. If you don't find your question here, don't hesitate to go over to the contact area and ask me directly. I hope that we are able to work together!

 

What do you specialize in?

I specialize in WordPress Development. That includes making, adapting and customizing WordPress themes and also Plugin Development.

What services do you offer?

I offer general web development services as well, such as Site Migrations, PSD to HTML conversions, script installations and much more!

How long does making a website take?

Every web project is different, for that reason I ask you to contact me so that I can quote you regarding your project. I like to deliver a high quality product, so the work will not just be done but we will work together to make sure it meets high standards.

Do you do UX and UI as well?

My education has covered fundamentals of UX and UI design, however it is not my expertise. I work with several UX/UI professionals, if you contact me I can refer you to someone that will be able to help you with these types of services.

If x/y/z CMS is free, why do you charge so much for developing a site?

Even though many of the popular content management systems are very easy to work with, many projects need special customization; not just to get a personalized product, but also so that you, the client, can fully manage your website.
