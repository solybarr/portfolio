Hugh Herr

Who?

Rock climber, engineer and biophysicist.

Why?

Hugh Herr has used his personal experience as fuel for an indomitable search for the betterment of bionics. My favorite quote of his is "With Bionics I am no longer Disabled".

Pink

Who?

SInger, songwriter, dancer and actress.

Why?

Pink has her own style and is not ashamed of it. She often uses her lyrics to protest about real events in her country and in the world. Some of my favorite songs of her are "Mr President" and "Stupid Girls". I like the latter because it tells women we are more than just a shell, we can be more.

Usain Bolt

Who?

Olympic athlete, regarded as the fastest human ever timed.

Why?

There was a picture of him all over Facebook at the Rio 2016 Olympics where it seemed running and winning was just an every day occurrence for this gentleman. I later saw a video where it shows how he trains with no days off and is constantly seeking his goal of being faster. Here is the link, to a facebook page of Muscle Madness magazine that shows the video and how his own voice says "I need to work Harder".
